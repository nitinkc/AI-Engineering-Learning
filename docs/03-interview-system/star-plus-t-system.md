# STAR+T System

## Framework

- Situation: context and constraints
- Task: your direct ownership
- Action: architecture, implementation, debugging, evaluation
- Result: measurable impact
- Tradeoffs: why your decision was correct for constraints

## 8-Story Bank

4. Improved latency and cost
