# STAR Stories Guide (Condensed)

Source baseline: `docs/00-prerequisites/star_stories_ai_engineer_reference.md`

## What Interviewers Look For

- Ownership
- Technical depth
- Production reliability
- Measurable impact

## STAR+T at Interview Speed

- Situation: context and constraints
- Task: scope and ownership
- Action: architecture, implementation, debugging, evals
- Result: technical + business impact with metrics
- Tradeoffs: alternatives and why rejected

## Recommended Story Bank

1. Production-style RAG build
2. RAG quality debugging
3. Agentic workflow and tool calling
4. Latency/cost optimization
5. Evals and monitoring
6. Production deployment
7. AI coding pilot and guardrails
8. Team playbook and standards

## Metrics to Include

- Retrieval hit rate, faithfulness, citation accuracy
- Task success rate, retry rate, escalation rate
- P95 latency, cost per successful task
- Time saved, ticket deflection, adoption lift

## 90-Second Formula

```text
Project -> problem -> ownership -> architecture -> hard part
-> fix -> measurable result -> production lesson
```
